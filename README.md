# Repo_Tosini
👋 Welcome to my AI & Data Science journey

This repo is the story of how I went from "what does print() even do" to building dashboards, training my Python brain, and shipping real projects — all during my Introduction to AI studies at Breda University of Applied Sciences (BUas).

Grab a coffee. Here's what's inside. ☕

🐍 Python, from zero to (almost) hero

I worked through the fundamentals the hard way — variables, conditionals, loops, functions, strings, lists, dictionaries, tuples, file handling — then leveled up into NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib for real data work. Plus a healthy dose of algorithms (yes, I now know what Big O means and I'm not afraid to use it).

🤖 AI in Science Fiction

Picked apart how AI shows up in sci-fi, mapped it to a real taxonomy of AI domains, assessed whether the tech is actually feasible (spoiler: sometimes no), and dug into the ethics. Equal parts nerdy and thought-provoking.

📊 Power BI & the SDG Dashboard

Built an interactive dashboard around a UN Sustainable Development Goal, following the full CRISP-DM pipeline — business understanding, data cleaning, exploratory analysis, evaluation, and a published end-user-ready product.

👥 Teamwork & real tooling

Collaborated on a group project using Git/GitHub for version control and Poetry for environment management. Branches, commits, merge conflicts, and the occasional "wait why won't my venv activate" — the real developer experience.

🎯 What I'm taking with me

Problem-solving over memorizing. Clean, readable code over clever code. And the confidence that I can pick up a new tool, break it a few times, and figure it out.

Thanks for stopping by — feel free to explore the folders! 📁
